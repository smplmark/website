Sources

Where smplmark's ingested benchmarks come from.

Some benchmarks on smplmark republish openly licensed results from third-party sources. They are imported directly from each source — which is why they carry a Verified publisher badge — and republished as facts, with attribution, exactly as published. When a source is retired or frozen, its benchmarks are marked complete. No source endorses smplmark.

OpenML citation: Vanschoren et al., "OpenML: networked science in machine learning," SIGKDD Explorations 15(2), 2013. smplmark is not affiliated with or endorsed by Stanford University, Hugging Face, or any other source.

Results are republished as facts, with attribution, and no source endorses smplmark. If you represent a source and want data corrected or removed, email support@smplmark.org — removals are fast.
